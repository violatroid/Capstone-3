Welcome to Your Space!
This is a site where you can feel in a safe space to vent. All users are required and expected to be respectful and curteous of one another as this is a free safe space to vent or to ask for advice.
About Us:
Mental Health is important! We are all human and it is important to talk to someone or vent to others to let you know that you are not alone. This is a safe and friendly community to vent and to give each other friendly advice to one another. Venting is a healthy way to process emotions, relieve stress, and to connect with others.
